About TB-500

TB-500 is a synthetic version of Thymosin Beta-4, a naturally occurring peptide present in nearly all human and animal cells. It is widely studied in research for its effects on tissue repair, recovery, inflammation, and angiogenesis in animal models.

TB-500 is commonly researched alongside BPC-157 in healing protocols. Quality varies between suppliers, so third-party HPLC and mass spectrometry testing is essential.
