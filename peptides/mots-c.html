About MOTS-c

MOTS-c (Mitochondrial Open Reading Frame of the 12S rRNA-c) is a 16-amino acid mitochondrial-derived peptide. It is studied in research for its role in metabolic homeostasis, insulin sensitivity, and exercise-induced adaptations in animal models.

MOTS-c is sold worldwide as a research-use-only (RUO) compound. Purity and consistency vary between suppliers, which is why third-party lab testing (HPLC, mass spectrometry) and certificates of analysis matter when comparing options.
