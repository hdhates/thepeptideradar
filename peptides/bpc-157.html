About BPC-157

BPC-157 (Body Protection Compound 157) is a synthetic peptide derived from a protective protein in human gastric juice. It is one of the most widely studied research peptides for tissue repair, gut barrier function, and tendon and ligament healing in animal models.

BPC-157 is sold worldwide as a research-use-only (RUO) compound. Quality and purity vary significantly between suppliers, which is why third-party lab testing (HPLC, mass spectrometry) and certificates of analysis matter.
