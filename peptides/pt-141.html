About PT-141

PT-141 (Bremelanotide) is a synthetic cyclic heptapeptide and a melanocortin receptor agonist with selectivity for MC4R. It is studied in research for its effects on central melanocortin signaling and sexual response endpoints.

PT-141 is sold worldwide as a research-use-only (RUO) compound. Purity and consistency vary between suppliers, which is why third-party lab testing (HPLC, mass spectrometry) and certificates of analysis matter when comparing options.

PT-141 vs Melanotan II

Both are melanocortin receptor agonists studied in research, but with different selectivity profiles:

  • Receptor selectivity — PT-141 is more selective for MC4R; Melanotan II is non-selective and also targets MC1R
  • Research focus — PT-141 is primarily studied for CNS-mediated sexual response; Melanotan II for melanogenesis and pigmentation endpoints
  • Half-life — both are short-acting research peptides; protocols differ between the two
  • Stability — both should be reconstituted with bacteriostatic water and stored refrigerated
