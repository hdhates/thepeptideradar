About Melanotan II

Melanotan II (MT-2) is a synthetic analog of alpha-melanocyte stimulating hormone (alpha-MSH). It is widely studied in research for melanogenesis (skin pigmentation) and other physiological effects in animal models.

Sold worldwide as a research compound, purity varies significantly between sources. Look for suppliers with HPLC verification and third-party certificates of analysis.
