Our process

How We Verify Suppliers

Every supplier on ThePeptideRadar goes through the same process before going live. No shortcuts, no exceptions.

1
We reach out directly
Every supplier is personally contacted by our team. No supplier can self-list — we initiate or verify every application before anything goes live.
2
We check lab testing
We verify whether suppliers offer third-party lab testing or Certificates of Analysis (COAs). Suppliers with verified testing get a badge on their listing.
3
We review community feedback
We cross-reference reviews from trusted platforms like Judge.me and community forums. Suppliers with a history of complaints or scam reports are not listed.
4
We verify shipping & operations
Shipping times, countries served, and operational status are all confirmed before listing. Outdated or inactive suppliers are removed.
5
Listings are monitored ongoing
Getting listed isn't permanent. We monitor suppliers over time and remove any that no longer meet our standards. You can report issues via our contact page.
Know a supplier we should list?
If you've had a good experience with a supplier that isn't on ThePeptideRadar, let us know and we'll look into it.
